More freedom, not less

A list of laws and customs that are fucked up and need to be fixed.

Curated by Soham Sankaran.

Preamble

I have a simple political philosophy: more freedom, not less. In particular, this means more freedom for the little guy, for the independent thinker, the protestor, the iconoclast, the conscientious objecter -- dissent must not be grounds for persecution, difference must not lead to discrimination.

I built this page after observing a repeated pattern of behaviour: I read or hear about some law or custom that obviously runs counter to those ideals, get worked up about it for a few days, then forget the exact details after I find the next fucked up thing. At the end of many, many iterations of this cycle, I'm left only with a lot of unresolved residual anger and have done very little to fix the underlying issues.

This list is an attempt to catalogue each of these fucked up things as I find them, as well as to track efforts to fix them.

If you have something to add to this list, email me at soham [at] soh (dot) am.

Let it breathe

Look at lines 1 and 2 of some shitty website you're building. Assuming they're not married they probably shouldn't be humping. The defaults are trash -- pick a minimum line-height: 1.4 for body copy. Headings should be tighter. If you can't see that...piss off.

A little less contrast

Black on white? How often do you see that kind of contrast in real life? Tone it down a bit, asshole. I would've even made this site's background a nice #EEEEEE if I wasn't so focused on keeping declarations to a lean 7 fucking lines.

Size Matters

I know your partner says otherwise, but it's true. Bump that body copy to render close to 16px or more. Smaller type works well for print, not the screen.

Line-width, motherfucker

Looking at an LCD screen is strainful enough. Don't make me read a line of text that's 200 fucking characters long. Keep it to a nice 60-80 and users might actually read more than one sentence of your worthless dribble.
